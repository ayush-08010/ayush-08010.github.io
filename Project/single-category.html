Specials



S01

Key Lime Pie Refresher

Order a Venti Kiwi Starfruit Refresher with no inclusions, add light coconut milk, then ask for vanilla sweet cream cold foam with one pump of funnel cake syrup blended in. Don’t forget the caramel crunch topping!

S02

Draco Malfoy Refresher

This Harry Potter-inspired Refresher starts off with a Venti Star Drink with no inclusions. Then, add 2 pumps of pineapple syrup, and ask for cold foam made with coconut milk along with one scoop of matcha powder

S03

Caribbean Refresher

This tropical beauty was created by Holly Walker, who is an absolute Starbucks mastermind. Start by ordering a Venti Mango Dragonfruit Lemonade with no inclusions, aka the scoop of dragonfruit that usually comes in this drink. Then, ask for light kiwi juice and peach juice. To create the ruby hues, ask for one pump of raspberry syrup and light passion tea to be poured on top of the drink.

S04

Strawberry Cobbler Refresher

Order a Venti Pink Drink with no inclusions, add 4 pumps of pineapple ginger syrup, and top it off with vanilla sweet cream cold foam with 2 pumps of pineapple ginger syrup and strawberry puree blended in

S05

Honey Peach Freeze

We’re feeling REAL peachy about this pairing. Order up a Venti Blended Lemonade with peach juice and a splash of guava juice. Ask your barista to strip up the inside of the cup with agave, then top with vanilla sweet cream foam and toasted honey topping. The best part? This tastes like summer no matter the season.

S06

Blueberry Muffin Frappuccino

Start by ordering a grande vanilla bean Frappuccino with one pump of hazelnut syrup and fresh blueberries blended in. The top is whipped cream, caramel crunch topping and a handful of fresh blueberries.
